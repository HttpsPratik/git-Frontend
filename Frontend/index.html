Why to Adopt and not Shop ?

1. ADOPTING MEANS YOU SAVE A LIFE!
When you adopt, not only do you save your loving new companion, but you make space for other animals who desperately needs it. You are providing a second chance to an animal in need. Many animals in streets are loving and deserving of a forever home, and by adopting, you directly contribute to saving a life.

2. YOU CAN'T BUY HAPPINESS, IT COMES TO YOU!
The biggest motto to remember here is “Money can't buy you happiness”. The love you receive from animals is unconditional. So whether you purchase a tiny puppy or you rescue an abandoned one, the degrees of love will be the same. They don't show the difference then why do we feel that buying the breed that you prefer might give you more happiness?

3. YOU WILL DIRECTLY NOT BE SUPPORTING ANIMAL CURELITY!
When you buy a dog or rabbit from a pet store, you're actually supporting the cruel puppy mills that are literally “breeding factories” that put profits way before the welfare of dogs. Animals from puppy mills are housed in shockingly poor conditions. Without any proper medical care, they fall sick often and are behaviourally troubled as a result.

4. THESE ANIMALS DESERVES A FOREVER HOME TOO!
As we humans want to be loved and find companionship in our family and friends, why should we take away that right from all these homeless, abandoned animals. They were treated wrongly, and not given this right to happiness and by giving them some space in our hearts and homes, we are giving them what they deserve!

5. YOU WILL ALWAYS FEEL PROUD FOR HAVING A LIFE SAVED!
Everyday, when you come back home from work or school, it's an ecstatic feeling for both you and your pet. They wait all day for you to come back home. A sense of joy takes over you when you realise that you actually made those little ones feel worthy, you’ll definitely feel pride seeping into your heart. The heart that chose an animal that needed a home rather than an animal that was created for homes.
